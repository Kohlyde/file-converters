Konnexion is Idaho’s largest electronic music and arts festival. We’re dedicated to building an amazing summer camping and festival experience while creating a space for everyone to express their true selves.

Our goal is to build community and celebrate music and art. We strive to provide a comfortable and truly unique experience, unlike any other in Idaho. Art installations and theme camps, food and retail vendors, and all your favorite people. We encourage everyone to make their camps unique and lit up. Camping with your vehicles in included in your ticket.

Blessing our stages will be a highly curated eclectic vision of melodic journeys. From Bass Music to Techno and everything in between. Aside from music, we feature performance artists, live painters and artistic sculptures throughout the event. Join us on this journey of creating space for like-minded individuals to gather, grow, and connect.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the 2024 event?

June 28th - 30th, 2024.

Gates open on Friday June 28th, 2024 at 10am.

Where is Konnexion located?

Konnexion is located just 45 minutes outside of Boise, Idaho. The festival takes place on private land in the mountains just outside of Horseshoe Bend.

Witness breathtaking views throughout the property, and experience music at our stage locations that are secluded yet still have spacious dancefloors.

For easy directions we suggest downloading our Mobile Application

Is car camping included?

YES! All camping and Car camping is included with all General Admission ticket purchases. No extra fees there. If you would like to bring a camper trailer or RV you will need an RV pass.

Bring your cars, build a big camp, we have plenty of space for you to set up your home away from home. If you would like to secure the best camping spots, we suggest getting a Thursday Entry Pass.

Where can I get tickets for Konnexion?

Tickets for 2024 go on sale on February 1st, 2024

Ticket prices have been lowered for 2024!

Can I come to Konnexion for one day?

Day passes are only available if the event does not sell out. We do not expect to have day passes available for 2024

If we do offer day passes, they will be available 2 weeks before the festival.

What kind of music will be at Konnexion 2024?

We feature a highly curated lineup of Local, Regional, Upcoming, and International acts. At Konnexion you will not hear the same type of music between the two stages at the same time
